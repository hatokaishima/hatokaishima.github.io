a self-study road to computer science
5 July 2024

primers must read, even we cannot grasp them in just one time, read as many times as possible:

  1. Code: The Hidden Language of Computer Hardware and Software(2nd)
  2. Computer Systems: A Programmer’s Perspective(3rd)
  3. SICP(2nd)
  4. The C Programming Language(2nd)
  5. The Scheme Programming Language(4th), Chez Scheme Version 10 user’s Guide (maybe later).
  6. How to Design Programs(2nd), the little schemer(4th) (maybe later).
  7. Python Programming: An Introduction to Computer Science(3rd)
  8. MDN (introductions to html, css, javascript)

tool:

  • buy a Windows laptop and reinstall latest Fedora on it.
  • or build a desktop pc with the hardwares you like (best with a 27 inch 4K display and a mechanical keyboard with red switches).
  • or just buy a Mac mini (best with Magic Trackpad 2).
  • an iPad (if possible)

code editor:

  • sublime text (with sublimeREPL)

others:

  • Typewriter (Markdown app for MacOS)
  • Apostrophe (Markdown app for linux)
